Dapper Day Returns to Disney Parks!

A bi-annual fan event that takes place every spring and fall, Dapper Day is one of the most popular fan events in the Disney Resorts.
This year Spring Dapper Day are April 22 and 23 at Disneyland, 28th and 29th at Magic Kindgom, April 30th at Epcot and May 6 at Disneyland Paris.

For more information check out the following article: Dapper Day returns to Disney parks for their annual Spring Outing
