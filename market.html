Those who love barbershop music will surely love these traditional street performers. They spend their time bringing joy to visitors of the DisneyLand and World Parks. Singing classic Disney toons they appeal to all ages and all lovers of all things disney. Their old pinstripes and straw hats add a feel of authenticity to Mainstreet, U.S.A. You can even get your haircut at a real barbershop as they serenade you! Fun for the entire family. They even had a few Dapper Dan groups in Disney's Hong Kong and UK locations! They are definitely an attraction for those who love music and Disney!
